☪️ Islamic Financing with EURX

Sharia-compliant financing structures. 0% riba (interest). Profit-sharing instead of interest.

📜 Available Structures

  • Murabaha — Cost-plus financing. We buy an asset and sell it to you at a markup. You pay in installments.
  • Mudaraba — Profit-sharing. You provide capital (EURX), we manage the investment. Profits shared 50/50.
  • Ijara — Lease-to-own. We buy an asset and lease it to you. Ownership transfers at the end.
  • Qard al-Hasan — Benevolent loan. Interest-free loan with no profit. Only admin fee (3%).

💰 Loan Terms

  • Term: up to 60 months (5 years)
  • Collateral: EURX (100–150%)
  • Application fee: 3% (non-refundable)
  • Insurance: 1–3% (refundable after full repayment)

📥 How to Apply

1. Complete KYC
2. Deposit collateral (EURX) to escrow address
3. Sign Islamic financing agreement
4. Receive financing in EURX or fiat
